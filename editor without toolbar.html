How to use Online Video Editor?

1

Upload Media

Select your video and audio files. Supported formats: MP4, MOV, WebM, MP3, WAV.

2

Customize Layout

Choose aspect ratio (16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok) and adjust zoom scale.

3

Add Effects & Text

Use the toolbar to add color filters, text overlays, watermarks, or freeze effects.

4

Export Video

Click "Start Processing" to render. The tool processes everything in your browser.

Why use this tool?

No Watermark 100% Private No Installation MP4 Output

Online Video Editor works entirely in your web browser using modern WebAssembly and Canvas APIs. Your video files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete data privacy and security. It is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.
